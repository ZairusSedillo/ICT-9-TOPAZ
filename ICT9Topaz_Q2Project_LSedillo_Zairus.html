Best Places in the Philippines

Boracay

Boracay in the Philippines is famous for its gorgeous beaches, silky white sand, clear blue water, and breathtaking sunsets. It's the top island in the country and has won many awards.

Manila

Manila, the capital of the Philippines, is the heart of the nation's economy, politics, culture, and social life. Situated on Luzon Island, it stretches by Manila Bay and the Pasig River.

Tagaytay

Tagaytay, a city in southwestern Luzon, Philippines, sits high on Tagaytay Ridge, about 2,000 feet above sea level. It's a popular weekend getaway known for its cool weather and awesome view of Taal Lake, home to an active volcano on an island.
